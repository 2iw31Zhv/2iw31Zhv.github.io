Ziwei Zhu (朱子微)




Welcome!

I got my PhD in Computer Science at Columbia University in the city of New York. I am now working at Synopsys Inc. as a R&D Engineer for the development of optical design softwares such as DiffractMOD and FullWAVE .

I studied at Columbia Computer Graphics Group with Professor Changxi Zheng . Before joining Columbia, I did research on first-principle calculation with Professor Lai Wang , and computer animation with Professor Bin Wang at Tsinghua University. I was also fortunate to intern at Meta/Facebook Reality Labs. I am interested in PDE, GPU and AI for Science in general.
